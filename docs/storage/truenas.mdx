Puddle is my NAS, running **TrueNAS SCALE** on a **45HomeLab HL15** with 256 GB
of RAM. It serves ZFS-backed NFS to the Kubernetes cluster (`puddle.outsideour.casa`).

## Pools

| Pool      | Role    | Drives                              | Layout        |
|-----------|---------|-------------------------------------|---------------|
| boot-pool | boot    | 1 × 1 TB Kingston NV3 NVMe          | Single        |
| puddle    | data    | 6 × 12 TB Seagate IronWolf / Exos   | 6-wide RAIDZ2 |
| puddle    | L2ARC   | 2 × 1.92 TB Samsung PM9A3 NVMe      | Cache         |
| puddle    | spare   | 1 × 12 TB Seagate IronWolf          | Hot spare     |

<Note>
A 750 GB Intel Optane drive is installed but currently unassigned — earmarked
for a future SLOG / special vdev.
</Note>
